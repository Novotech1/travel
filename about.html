ABOUT NOVOTECH TRAVEL AND TOUR COMPANY

Over the past 30 years, NOVOTECH travel and Tours Ltd, has evolved to become one of the most reputable Travel Management Companies in Ghana. Registered as an IATA agency since its inception in 1987, NOVOTECH has gone on to serve countless local and multinational companies in addition to winning almost every award within the Airline and Hospitality industry.

NOVOTECH has also held partnerships with Global Travel Management Companies; including Thomas Cook, Business Travel International (BTI) and Hogg Robinson Group (HRG). As part of the company’s vision to be the market leader in Ghana, NOVOTECH is currently investing in market leading technology to drive personalisation and exquisite guest experience for clients in diverse industries.

OUR MISSION

We are committed to offering travel services of the highest quality, combining our energy and enthusiasm, with our years of experience.
